Welcome to the Basics of Solitaire!

This lesson will teach you the basic knowledge needed to play Solitaire.  You will also learn how to play two of the most popular versions of the game, Canfield and Klondike Solitaire.

Each unit builds off the knowledge you gain in the previous units.  A quick check quiz at the end of each unit will let you assess your knowledge throughout the course.  You must pass each quick check quiz before continuing to the next unit. 

Continue to the introduction lesson to get started.
